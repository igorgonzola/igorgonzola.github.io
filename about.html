About Me

My name is Igor Zajac and I was born in Poland on July 30th, 1998. After having spent most of my early childhood in Zielona Gora, I moved to Wisbech in 2007 where I continued my education, finishing with 13 C-A* GCSEs and a DMM Computing BTEC. After this, I moved to Northampton to do a BSc in Software Engineering.

History in Computing

My interest in programming began with Macromedia Flash. I realised I was more interested in programming than art after finding out that I had more fun messing around with ActionScript than actually animating. From this point on, I started experimenting more with basic HTML, CSS and Python, later moving on to small projects in Unity using C#. After finishing my BSc, I plan on finding a career in software engineering.
